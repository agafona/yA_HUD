These files go into your /resource/ui folder.

The labels on the scoreboard can be customized at the top of the scoreboard.res file.

An alternative version of the SpecHud with HP crosses is included, you will need to remove the " - ALLCAPS" extension before moving the files into your resource folder. The "SpectatorTournamentGUIHealth - CROSS.res" file works for both cross variants of the SpecHUD (top + bottom).

These files also move the koth time panel next to the cp and make the text on the TargetIDs slightly larger.
