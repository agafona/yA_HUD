Move the two captions files from one of the versions into your resource folder in /tf/custom/yourfoldername/resource to enable captions. 

Their location and their behaviour can be customized in /scripts/hudlayout.res.

-----------------------

You can find the original and full captions here: https://code.google.com/p/tf2captions/
Here's a tutorial for customizing: http://teamfortress.tv/forum/thread/8335-custom-caption-tutorial
